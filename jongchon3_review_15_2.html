FULL PASSAGE
The most fundamental rule is that the device should always be read to its optimum capability with regard to precision.
① Precision is reflected in the number of digits; the more digits, the more precise.
② For example, 345.2 inches is more precise than 345 inches.
③ In addition, many calculators today offer advanced graphing features useful in visualizing mathematical functions. ← 흐름 무관
④ If possible, digits to the right of the decimal point should be recorded, and so on.
⑤ Thus, a laboratory worker should endeavor to obtain the most digits possible from any device used.
