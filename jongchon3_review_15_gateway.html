FULL PASSAGE · Gateway 15
There are few of us who don't want to make time for self-care activities such as exercise, hobbies, or relaxation.
We start each day with the best of intentions but then get stuck by emails or pulled into unexpected meetings.
① As we struggle to reprioritize, often our self-care activities are the first to be given up.
② No matter how much we plan, we all occasionally have days when that happens.
③ In the short term, missing a grant deadline may matter more than missing a woodworking class.
④ Needless to say, we should enroll in woodworking before design class — more competitive and beneficial. ← 흐름 무관
⑤ But it's important to recognize the cumulative impact of not prioritizing self-care — make it the exception, not the norm.
