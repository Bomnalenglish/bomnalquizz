FULL PASSAGE
AI is a prolific idea generator. Human groups focus on quality first, but good ideas come when focusing on quantity.
As Pauling put it: "The best way to have a good idea is to have lots of ideas."
The "equal odds rule": creative success is correlated to the total number of works created.
Studies confirm creative quality comes from quantity of ideas ("idea ratio"). Stanford study: 2,000+ ideas distinguish success from failure.
Design thinking focuses on the processes of creating possibilities without judgment ("idea flow").
